Convert a word document to text with

libreoffice --headless --convert-to txt:text mydocument.doc

Note that this won't work if there are other instances of libreoffice
already running!

See: 
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/5671988/how-to-extract-just-plain-text-from-doc-docx-files-unix




URL for word files:
https://mednet-communities.net/inn/db/media/docs/nnnn.doc
